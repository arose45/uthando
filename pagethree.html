Seattle

There are many places to fish near Seattle: Belvoir Place, Green Lake Park, and Fairview Park are among the few to name. Steelhea, chinook, and salmon can be caught in the area.

If you happen to have bad luck while fishing, you can still visit Pikes Place.

Seattle is home to areas where massive salmon can be caught.
